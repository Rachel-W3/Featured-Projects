The Legend of

Rupee Hunter

Welcome, dear Zelda fans and newcomers. Have you ever wondered what the Hero of Time does when he's not saving Princess Zelda and Hyrule from Ganondorf's corruption? Well, you probably already guessed it -- he prances around a grassy field collecting rupees.

This is a single-player game where the player controls the Hero of Time, AKA Link, and walks around a field surrounded by a border of spikes to collect as many rupees as possible without running into the spikes. Each rupee will add 10 points. Fair warning -- every 100 points will increase the difficulty.
